  Personal Story
During my junior year, I suffered a double bone fracture as a student-athlete and fainted from the injury. I was mistakenly taken to Emory ER, where I waited over 12 hours for surgery. As an international student, my first experience with the U.S. healthcare system was overwhelming, fragmented, and unsuccessful.

Problem
College students face medical situations with no guidance - poor triage, missed campus resources, a bill they never saw coming, no one translating the system for them. Not because the care isn't there, but because they can't navigate to it.

Solution
MedEase is an Agentic AI platform built for college studnets navigating injuries, emergencies, and recovery. It combines specialized AI agents with real-time institutional data to understand a student's situation, connect them to the right resources, and walk them through what to do next - on campus and beyond.
