# North Star

> Last updated: 2026-03-20
> Agreed by: Rolf, Annie

## What MedEase Is

MedEase is an accessibility-focused healthcare assistant for Emory
students — specifically those navigating Emory's Disability and
Accessibility Services (DAS). It simplifies medical documentation,
answers accommodation questions through a multi-agent RAG chat system,
and helps caregivers stay informed.

## Who It's For

Primary: Emory students with disabilities navigating DAS processes.
Secondary: Caregivers and family members supporting those students.

## What Success Looks Like

A student who previously found the DAS accommodation process confusing
and overwhelming can use MedEase to understand their options, prepare
their documentation, and feel supported — without needing to make a
phone call or read through walls of institutional text.

## What MedEase Is Not

- Not a replacement for medical professionals or DAS advisors
- Not a general-purpose healthcare chatbot
- Not scoped beyond Emory in V1
